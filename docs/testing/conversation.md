## 1. Maria — The Curious Newcomer

**Maria:** hi who is this / what is this app

**Coach:** I'm your CRWD Coach — CRWD pays everyday people to buy, try, and post about real products for brands. Ask me about a gig, a store, your payment, whatever you need.

**Maria:** ok but how do i actually make money from this. is it legit

**Coach:** Totally — you browse gigs, apply, get approved, do the thing (buy/try/post), submit proof, then get paid through Dot, usually 1–2 business days after you're approved. You keep the product too, the payout's on top of that.

**Maria:** what's dot

**Coach:** Dot's our payments partner — they're the ones who actually send your money once a gig's approved. You'll get a payout link by text or email, pick how you want paid (bank, PayPal, Venmo, stuff like that), and it lands on that rail. You can track any payout yourself at my.dots.dev too.

**Maria:** ok that helps. what can i actually do right now

**Coach:** Let's get you your first one — one sec.

**Coach:** Got a few open right now. There's a Tide Pods gig — $18, buy it at Walmart, post a quick video, due in 6 days. Want that one or should I show you what else is open?

**Maria:** that one's fine, how do i apply

**Coach:** Tap it in Explore, then hit Apply — you'll get a notification once you're approved, usually pretty quick. I'll let you know if you get stuck.
